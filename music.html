R&B

One genre I listen to is R&B. R&B music is a genre of music that originated in the 1940s from African-Americans. It is characterized by smooth rhythms, blending elements of jazz, gospel, and blues in its music. One of my favorite R&B songs currently is Luther by Kendrick Lamar & SZA.
